# IN_PRAISE_OF_TIME

IN_PRAISE_OF_TIME is Dai Pan's personal 2023-2026 browser collection: a record of waiting, displacement, moving without arriving, and arriving without recognizing the place.

The website behaves like an old desktop system. It contains windows, search panels, games, a monitor, a profiler, a Finder-like file browser, a calendar, a dock, text documents, photographs, videos, coordinates, and signal metadata. The interface is part of the work: it is not only a container for the archive.

## Chapters

CHAPTER 00 / LOCATING

Coordinates, boot sequence, shadow, delay, and the archive locating itself before any file is fully opened. This chapter introduces the idea that time is found through incomplete signals.

CHAPTER 01 / THE SAME WINDOW

New York, winter, repeated windows, rooms, night recordings, and an interior life seen through glass. The same window is recorded until it becomes less like a view and more like a witness.

CHAPTER 02 / NO SIGNAL

Daqing, Qiqihar, Arxan, grassland, borders, and the loss of GPS. This chapter treats maps as unreliable, especially when the landscape is too large or too green to accept a border.

CHAPTER 03 / HOMETOWN

Nantong, Huai'an, Changshu, low altitude, ordinary life, family tables, hand-brew coffee, and return. Hometown is not presented as a pin on a map, but as a quieter arrangement of light, water, rooms, and repeated domestic evidence.

CHAPTER 04 / ASCENT

Qinghai and Mt. Tai appear as separated journeys inside one symbolic altitude chapter. The graph is not a scientific chart; it is a visual guide for expectation, fatigue, altitude, and the measurable weight of travel.

CHAPTER 05 / BRISBANE

Brisbane, river light, commute fragments, Morningside, Ascot, Victoria, and Melbourne as an Australian extension. The chapter watches repeated movement until routine starts looking like evidence.

CHAPTER 06 / AFTERIMAGE

Pentax, Ricoh, Canon, black-and-white photography, coffee, flowers, rooms, beds, desktops, and the residue after travel. The camera remains after the location recedes.

CHAPTER AFTER / TXT

A final text file that keeps the archive open after the visible chapters end.
